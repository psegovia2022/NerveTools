What can we help you build today?

Nerve Tools is an open-source project that aims to democratize access to science. The ultimate goal is to assist scientists, especially in undeveloped countries, to acquire, build, and design bio instruments if needed.

About Us

We develop specialized tools to record the electrical activity in the extracellular nerve tissue from behaving animals. As well as to perturb such activity to explore causal hypotheses about how neural networks work.
