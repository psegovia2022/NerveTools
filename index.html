What can we help you build today?

Nerve Tools is an open source project that aims to democratizing access to science. The ultimate goal is to assist scientists specially in undeveloped countries to acquire, build, and design, if needed it, bioinstruments.

The Products page shows our catalogue of parts that are available to download for free.

About Us

We develope specialized tools to record extracellularly the electric activity in the nerve tissue from behaving animals. As well as, to perturb such activity to explore causal hypothesis about how neural networks work.

Marcelo Aguilar-Rivera

Founder & CTO

Nerve Tools was founded by Marcelo Aguilar-Rivera. Dr. Aguilar-Rivera earned a doctorate from the Physiological Science Program at Pontificie Universidad Católica de Chile.

Dr. Aguilar Rivera is a neuroscientist with more than a decade of experience in electrophysiology and bioinstrumentation in world-class research institutes.
