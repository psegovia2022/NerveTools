Nerve Tools is an open source project that aims to democratize access to science. The ultimate goal is to assist scientists specially in undeveloped countries to acquire, build, and design, if needed it, bioinstruments.

About Us

We develope specialized tools to record extracellularly the electric activity in the nerve tissue from behaving animals. As well as, to perturb such activity to explore causal hypothesis about how neural networks work.
