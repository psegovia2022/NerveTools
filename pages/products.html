Small Manipulator

This device has been used to record extracellular activity of single units and field potentials in the prefrontal-hippocampal circuit of behaving mice (Negr√≥n-Oyarzo et al. 2018).

Also, we record single cells activity and field potentials using tetrodes from behaving rats. Aditionally, the same device was used as the physical platform to hold electrodes to deploy electric micro-stimulation (Aguilar-Rivera et al.2020).

You can find a detailed description of this manipulator in both publications.

If you download and use it, please cite the publication that better match your research.

We have successfully printed the parts of this device using "smooth fine detail plastic," "white processed versatile" and "black Hi-Def acrylate."
